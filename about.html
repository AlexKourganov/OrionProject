What We Do

The Orion company was created in early 2001 as a solution to energy crysis. During that time the Orion scientist discovered a new way to generate power. Codenamed Musk engine, the new engine was capable to generate more then enough power to provide entire world with elictricity for generations to come.

At that point we started to wonder what else can we do with all that power. And it was then when a todays Orion was born. In early 2020 a first space ship fitted with Musk engine was build. It was able to reach the speed close to the speed of light. Travel to Mars became possible in less then an hour. At this point Orion is at frontier of exploring and colonizing Mars.
